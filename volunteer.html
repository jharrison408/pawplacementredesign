Join Our Team

We offer a range of volunteering options based on your interests, availability, and capability. From a few hours a month playing with animals at events, to the ultimate commitment of fostering a mama and her litter, we have a way you can help.

Adoption Representatives

Help our kitties find homes during our scheduled adoption events. Weekday evening and weekend shifts available.

Cat and Dog Fosters

Every time you choose to foster a dog or cat, you are saving a life. Paw Placement provides food, bedding, toys, and veterinary care for your foster pet. You just need to provide a safe, loving home to the dog or cat, and transport them to vet appointments and adoption events.

Kitty Caretakers

Our kitty caretaker volunteers feed, cuddle, and clean up after our PetSmart Adoption Center cats. We need kitty caretakers for morning and evening shifts every single day of the week. The shifts are usually 1 to 2 hours, so you should be able to fit it in to your schedule.

Dog Adoption Event Volunteers

We need your help wrangling puppies and walking dogs at our Saturday adoption events!

You must be available 10:45AM-1 PM or 1 PM to 3:15 PM on Saturdays.
