Donate to Paw Placement

Your donations help us rescue more lives.

Why Donate?

Paw Placement's life-saving programs are only possible through public donations. Every penny matters. Please support Paw Placement and all of the animals who need our help.

Monthly or Single Donation

Monthly and single contributions offer a reliable source of income for our cats and dogs. You can make an impact with any monthly donation.

Give Every Time You Shop on Amazon

When you shop on Amazon, Amazon donates!

Donate an Item on Our Wish List

There are items that help us keep our rescued animals safe, clean and happy. Donations are greatly appreciated and can be dropped off at our Cat Center located inside the PetSmart on Shea and 90th st in Scottsdale, Arizona.
