Our Vet Partners

Paw Placement is proud to work with several veterinary clinics in the Valley.

These vets go the extra mile to support animal rescue by providing us with affordable and compassionate care. We highly recommend the below vets:

Vet Clinics

Kierland Animal Clinic

We have worked with Dr. Krista Gibson for many years.

She and her staff are all amazing and compassionate people. We bring our newly rescued cats to her to get them checked out and on their way to a new home.

Behavior Specialist

Tamara Verhas, Behavior Specialist

Your home is the place where your dog feels the most comfortable and where most behavior issues develop. As a Dog Behavior Specialist, Tammy with Pawsitive Energy interacts with you and your dog in your personal space to evaluate behaviors and identify their root causes.

We highly recommend Tamara Verhas for any behavioral training with your cat or dog.
